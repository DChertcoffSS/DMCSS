FAR Compliance

Build proposals that are complete, compliant, and compelling.

The Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) sets the rules of federal procurement. A compliant proposal is table‑stakes for award consideration; a compelling proposal wins.

Core Principles

  • Fairness & Transparency: Follow the solicitation instructions precisely (Sections L & M).
  • Traceability: Map every requirement to your response; avoid orphan content.
  • Completeness: Provide all certifications and representations (Section K) and required attachments (Section J).
  • Consistency: Align price, technical, and management volumes; reconcile assumptions.

Best Practices

  • Use a compliance matrix against Sections C, L, and M.
  • Mirror solicitation terminology and numbering.
  • Call out benefits, features, and proof with customer‑centric outcomes.
  • Validate pricing structures and CLINs (Section B) for accuracy.
  • Apply quality controls for deliverables and acceptance (Section E).

What We Deliver

  • Compliance matrix and proposal templates.
  • Volume outlines aligned to Sections L & M.
  • Editorial and red‑team review cycles.
  • Submission readiness and checklisting.
